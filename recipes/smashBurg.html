Smash Burgers!

Tools Required

  • Parchment paper
  • Frying pan
  • Spatula
  • Chef's knife (for chopping vegetables)
  • Cutting board
  • Ice-cream scoop (not require but nice to make similar sized patties)

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Hamburger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Pickles
  • Salt
  • Papper
  • Mayonase

*Note: you may or may not already have some of these in your pantry or refrigerator

Steps

  1. Use the ice-cream scoop to portion ground beef into golf ball sized balls. Rolling the meat into balls in the plam of your hands works well too
  2. Between two pieces of parchment paper, smash the balls of meat as thin as possible into patties.
    • There is also the option of smashing the burgers directly on the pan. By pre-smashing the patties between parchment paper, you can prep multiple burgers before beginning to cook.
  3. Carefully slice the onion and tomatoes into full, thin slices
  4. Chop the lettuce into strips by peeling a few leaves off and rolling them into a tube like fashion and then dicing.
  5. Set veggies aside until constructing the burger
  6. Pre-heat the pan on medium
  7. Melt a tbsp of butter and place the buns flat side down into the pan. This will get them nice and toasty!
  8. Once your buns are toasted, set them aside and put a healty smear of mayo on the bottom bun followed by lettuce then desired toppings.
  9. Melt a tbsp of butter and place a patty into the pan
  10. Salt and pepper exposed patty to taste
  11. Flip after patty is seared (~2 minutes give or take. They are thin and cook fast!)
  12. Let patty cook for ~2 minutes or until seared a golden brown
  13. Remove patty from pan and place directly on awaiting bun
  14. Top cooked patty with cheese
  15. Place a 2nd patty into the pan
  16. Salt and pepper exposed patty to taste
  17. Flip after patty is seared (~2 minutes give or take. They are thin and cook fast!)
  18. Top patty with cheese as it finishes cooking in pan
  19. Place the 2nd patty on top of previously cooked patty
  20. Top with top bun and enjoy!

At this point feel free to spice it up and customize it to your liking. Add mustard and ketchup, bacon, anything you like! I hope you enjoy this recipe!
