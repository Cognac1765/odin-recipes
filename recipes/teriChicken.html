Teriyaki Chicken!

*Note: this is a recipe for grilled teriyaki chicken. You can easily bake the same recipe at 375 degrees for 15 to 17 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 165 degrees following the same preperation instructions.*

Tools Required

  • Cutting board
  • Chef's knife
  • Plastic storage bag
  • Baking pan
  • Tin foil
  • Grill tongs
  • Probe thermometer
  • Non-Stick spray

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Soy-Vey Island Teriyaki marinade and sauce

*Note: you may or may not already have some of these in your pantry or refrigerator

Steps

  1. On a cutting board, trim fatty bits off of your chicken breasts
  2. With the knife in your right hand, place your left palm flat on the chicken breast
  3. Slice on the smooth, rounded side in the middle, laterally, almost all the way through the breast
  4. Fold open the chicken breast and further slice it until it opens flat
    • This is known as "butterflying" a chicken breast. It will help for quicker, more even cooking.
  5. Place the butterflyed chicken into the plastic storage bag
  6. Pour Soy-Vey Island Teriyaki marinade and sauce or teriyaki marinade of your choice generously over the chicken in the storage bag
  7. Let marinade at least 30 minutes and/or up to 24 hours
  8. Pre-heat grill to 450
  9. While the grill is pre-heating, line a baking pan with tin foil. This will help with clean up later!
  10. Gather the foil lined baking pan, grill tongs, probe thermometer, and non-stick spray
  11. Spry the grill with non-stick spray
  12. Gather the plastic storage bag with the marinaded chicken
  13. Place chicken on the grill
  14. Grill chicken 10 to 15 minutes flipping every 5 to 6 minutes until an internal temperature reads 160 degrees. Don't worry, the chicken will continue to cook even after its taken off the grill!
  15. Once the chicken is internally 160 degrees, remove from heat
  16. Place another sheet of tinfoil over the heated chicken to let it rest and continue to cook and stay warm
  17. After resting 5 minutes, slice into strips and serve!
